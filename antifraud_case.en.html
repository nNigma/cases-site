Case / Anti-fraud

Anti-fraud in monetization & advertising at a video platform

Building anti-fraud solutions to detect view inflation and ad fraud: from MVP scoring of channels/videos to event-level blocking and preparing near real-time anti-fraud.

Focus IVT / SIVT / GIVT
Format algorithms + product + process
Tools Python · SQL · Streamlit

Context

The task came with no clear goals or definitions. I gathered the requirements, agreed the success criteria and effectiveness metrics with the business, dug into the data, and started iterative development.

Role & collaboration

  • Working with stakeholders: monetization, moderation, commerce and advertising
  • Liaising with verification partners, handling complaints and appeals
  • Leading a group of web developers, data engineers and systems analysts

Key achievements

  • View-inflation detection algorithms: mathematical modeling and engineering
  • Web apps for fast channel checks (Streamlit)
  • Ad-fraud event detection: shift from blocking channels to blocking events
  • Experiments to validate effectiveness and calibrate IVT across all traffic
  • Productionization: MR/code review, testing, CI/CD

Timeline

1.5 months

MVP and first measures

  • MVP scoring of channels/videos (first version of the algorithms)
  • Introduced a payout "freeze" to prevent withdrawal of inflated earnings
  • Manual measures: disabling ads, terminating monetization contracts (manual)
  • Handling complaints and appeals (manual)
2.5 months

Stronger algorithms and going on the offensive

  • Improved algorithms; vetting incoming monetization applications
  • Defending the project at C-level, weekly reports
  • Streamlit web app for channel checks (built entirely by me)
  • Designed an automated contract-termination flow (cross-system)
5 months

IP scoring and ad-event anti-fraud

  • IP scoring + third-party data sources
  • Fraud classification: SIVT / GIVT / IVT
  • Hiring and growing the team (3 analysts)
  • Blocking fraudulent ad-impression events by IP
> 8 months

Move to ML and near real-time

  • Started the move to ML-based anti-fraud
  • Building our own anti-fraud platform; preparing near real-time
  • Large-scale channel amnesty; reconciliation and calibration against verifier benchmarks
  • Experiments to calibrate IVT across all traffic
